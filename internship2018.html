    Internship Program
    2018 Summer

Overview

IDEO Shanghai is offering a summer internship for talented Chinese students who are interested in exploring a career of creating positive impact through design.

We will provide you with a rapid guided learning experience that enables you to practice, experiment with, and master a variety of skills. Our people, our process, and our design-led projects will inspire you to imagine new possibilities for your career.

Our internship is two months long, and will begin in early July.

GET YOUR HANDS DIRTY

The bulk of the program is centered around fast-paced projects at IDEO Shanghai. You may work on a project to redefine the mission of an international brand in China; paint a vision for the future kitchen; or even design a new venture from scratch. Every intern is expected to be a contributing team member along the entire design thinking process.

ENGAGE A CREATIVE NETWORK

We believe that young talent are best inspired when they can meet new people who can become new role models. As an intern, you will meet a variety of talented people from the IDEO creative network, and have the chance to broaden your perspective by speaking with designers who come from wildly diverse backgrounds.

LEARN, REFLECT, & SHINE

As part of the program, you will have several mentors and guides to help you along your journey. We will provide a safe space for you to test out personal projects that showcase your passion and creativity, and provide advice on things like IDEO’s culture, skill development, and potential future career paths.

YOU ARE ...


SOMEONE WHO BRINGS YOUR WHOLE SELF TO WORK

We want to get inspired by you! You are someone who inspires us to look at the world through a different lens. If you have any unique skills, experiences, or points of view, please let us know! IDEO’ers are more than just designers, we are also documentarians, drummers, and stand-up comedians.

PASSIONATE ABOUT DESIGN THINKING

We are looking for students who understand the fundamentals of our methodology, and who are passionate about applying human-centered design to business challenges. Regardless of whether you are studying design, the social sciences, literature, or finance, you should have a strong interest in using your skills to drive social and business innovation in China.

HOW TO APPLY

Send your resume, a cover letter that includes answers to the below 2 questions, and any other materials that can help us to know you better to chinacareers@ideo.com with “Summer Internship” in the subject line.

The deadline for submission is June 10, 2018. We look forward to hearing from you!

1) What you are most passionate about and why?
2) What are some of the unique things (e.g. skills, POV, network) you think you can bring to IDEO Shanghai?
